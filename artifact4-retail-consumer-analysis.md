# Online Retail Consumer Preferences Analysis

## Introduction

This project analyzed online retail customer reviews and purchasing behavior to understand consumer preferences and buying patterns.

## Description

The project utilized customer review data and transaction records to identify product trends, customer sentiment, and regional purchasing differences.

## Objective

To uncover consumer behavior patterns and generate insights that support marketing, pricing, and product strategy decisions.

## Process

1. Collected customer review and transaction data.
2. Cleaned and prepared datasets for analysis.
3. Performed exploratory data analysis.
4. Applied sentiment analysis and natural language processing techniques.
5. Identified purchasing trends and regional preferences.
6. Created visual reports and dashboards.
7. Presented business recommendations.

## Tools and Technologies Used

- Python
- Pandas
- NLP
- Scikit-Learn
- Tableau
- Power BI

## Value Proposition

### Unique Value

Demonstrates customer analytics, text mining, sentiment analysis, and business intelligence skills.

### Relevance

Relevant to marketing analytics, customer insights, product management, and business analyst roles.
